Memory Game

How to Play

Flip two cards at a time. If they match, they stay open.

If not, they flip back after 1 second. Continue until all pairs are matched.

Rules

Only two cards can be flipped at once.

A match scores points; a mismatch counts as an attempt.

The fewer attempts, the better the score.

Benefits of Playing

Improves memory power.

Enhances concentration.

Fun and stress-relieving activity.

About Us

We are 2nd year CSE students from Kathir College of Engineering.
